## How to use this
Don't clone the repos into this folder — too much bulk. Open each README, pull the question **titles** into the matching `theory/interview-qs/<topic>.md` file, and write your own answers in your own words. Writing it yourself is the actual prep; copy-pasting answers isn't.

- Language/stack-specific (java.md, go.md, springboot.md) → personal `theory/interview-qs/`
- Stack-agnostic (react.md, javascript.md, oop.md, system-design.md) → `common/theory/interview-qs/`, since both of you need them
